Thumbnail sketches

SoupClub, a social food trading exchange.

  • Linear homepage

    A large hero shot (or image carousel) grabs the user's attention and has a quick visual explanation of the concept. A Sign Up button is placed underneath for immediately interested users. This also acts as a spacer before the content below. allowing for white space along the sides which draws the user's gaze down.

  • Grid homepage

    The hero shot in this one is halved, still prominent, but allowing for content to the side. The user reads from the left, and their attention is drawn to the largest image. Reading to the right they get a brief textual explanation of the service, followed by a small series of images explaining the process.

  • Member profile page

    The member's profile picture acts as a focal point at the top left corner, with it being the largest graphic on the page to emphasize the social nature of the site. Appearing below as part of the profile pic's visual area is the member's rating for site visitors to get immediate feedback on them. A small text area to the right of the profile picture contains a short snippet of the written by the member. Below are discreet member-selected tags that shows their specialties as categories, eg: gluten-free, southern, asian
